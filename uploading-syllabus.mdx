## Upload Process

Uploading your syllabus is the first step to creating a personalized academic plan with DormWay. Our system supports multiple file formats and automatically extracts key information to build your study schedule.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Prepare Your File" icon="file">
    Ensure your syllabus is in PDF, JPG, or PNG format and clearly readable.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Upload Page" icon="upload">
    Navigate to the upload section in your DormWay dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select File" icon="file-text">
    Choose your syllabus file from your device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Process Upload" icon="settings">
    Click upload and wait for AI processing to complete.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Plan" icon="eye">
    Review the generated academic plan and make any adjustments.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info">
  Processing typically takes 30-60 seconds depending on syllabus complexity.
</Callout>

## Supported Formats

DormWay accepts various file formats to accommodate different syllabus types:

| Format | Description | Max Size |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| PDF    | Scanned or digital PDFs | 10 MB   |
| JPG    | Image files               | 5 MB    |
| PNG    | High-quality images       | 5 MB    |

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="PDF Upload" icon="file-text">
    For digital PDFs, upload directly without additional processing.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Image Upload" icon="image">
    For scanned syllabi, ensure text is clear and legible.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mobile Upload" icon="smartphone">
    Use your phone's camera to capture syllabus pages.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<CodeGroup tabs="cURL,JavaScript">
  ```bash
curl -X POST \
  https://api.dormway.com/v1/syllabi \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
  -F "file=@syllabus.pdf" \
  -F "course_name=Computer Science 101"
  ```
  ```javascript
const formData = new FormData();
formData.append('file', syllabusFile);
formData.append('course_name', 'Computer Science 101');

fetch('/api/syllabi', {
  method: 'POST',
  headers: {
    'Authorization': `Bearer ${apiKey}`
  },
  body: formData
}).then(response => response.json());
  ```
</CodeGroup>

## Best Practices

To get the most accurate results from DormWay's AI processing:

- Use high-resolution scans or digital PDFs
- Ensure all text is clearly visible and not obscured
- Include course name, instructor, and key dates in the upload
- Review and correct any AI-extracted information
