## Getting Started

DormWay is an innovative tool designed to help students stay organized throughout their semester. By uploading their syllabus, students receive personalized academic plans that keep them on track and prevent falling behind. This free service is available to students at thousands of schools, offering a simple and intuitive interface for academic success.

DormWay transforms syllabus management into actionable plans, breaking down courses into manageable milestones and deadlines. Whether you're a freshman navigating your first semester or a senior balancing multiple courses, DormWay provides the structure you need to excel academically.

## How It Works

DormWay uses advanced AI to parse syllabus content and extract key information such as assignment deadlines, exam dates, and course requirements. The system then generates a comprehensive study plan that breaks down your semester into weekly and daily tasks.

  ```python
import requests

syllabus_data = {
  'course_name': 'Introduction to Computer Science',
  'assignments': [
    {'name': 'Project 1', 'due_date': '2024-02-15'},
    {'name': 'Midterm Exam', 'due_date': '2024-03-10'}
  ]
}

response = requests.post('/api/syllabi', json=syllabus_data)
print(response.json())
  ```
