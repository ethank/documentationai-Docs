## Viewing Your Plans

Once your syllabus is uploaded and processed, DormWay generates a comprehensive academic plan that breaks down your semester into manageable tasks and milestones.

## Plan Components

Each academic plan includes several key components:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Weekly Schedule" icon="calendar">
    Overview of assignments and due dates by week.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Study Tasks" icon="book-open">
    Daily study recommendations and reading assignments.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Progress Tracking" icon="check-circle">
    Mark completed tasks and monitor your advancement.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reminders" icon="bell">
    Automated notifications for upcoming deadlines.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Regularly update your plan to reflect completed work and changing priorities.
</Callout>

## Editing Plans

DormWay allows you to customize your generated plans to better fit your learning style and schedule.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Add Tasks" icon="plus">
    Include additional assignments or study sessions.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Modify Deadlines" icon="calendar">
    Adjust due dates based on your availability.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Prioritize Items" icon="star">
    Mark high-priority tasks for focused attention.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<CodeGroup tabs="JavaScript,Python">
  ```javascript
const planUpdate = {
  taskId: 'assignment-1',
  completed: true,
  notes: 'Completed ahead of schedule'
};

fetch(`/api/plans/${planId}/tasks/${taskId}`, {
  method: 'PUT',
  headers: {
    'Content-Type': 'application/json',
    'Authorization': `Bearer ${apiKey}`
  },
  body: JSON.stringify(planUpdate)
});
  ```
  ```python
import requests

plan_update = {
  'task_id': 'assignment-1',
  'completed': True,
  'notes': 'Completed ahead of schedule'
}

response = requests.put(
  f'/api/plans/{plan_id}/tasks/{task_id}',
  json=plan_update,
  headers={'Authorization': f'Bearer {api_key}'}
)
print(response.json())
  ```
</CodeGroup>
